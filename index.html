Games I have Made

There are 2 games I'm moderately proud of, one is a short turn-based dungeon crawler, the other one is a escape game.

The Great Escape

It is a simple dungeon crawler where your main goal is to get to the end goal which is a big doorway. On the way to getting to the goal, the player will be attacked through random encounters where they have to fight to continue, if they run out of hp, they will lose the game but if they win then they will earn experience points. If they earn enough experience points then they will lvl up which will restore their HP and MP to continue fighting.

The camera controls I have taken from how older dungeon crawlers like smt on NES or etrian odyssey on the DS did it, a fixed 90 degree turn in either left or right direction.


The Unknown Tower

For this game, I decided with making a lava room that rises every few seconds while the player tries to run away to unlock the door before escaping through it, should they not do it fast enough, then they will die and restart the level.

For the other levels it's more simple like the first level, it's 2 levels in one and jumping through a certain block will swap between level 1 and level 2.

last 2 levels are more to showcase the slimes, it has basic ai from patroling, to chasing player, dying, then heading back to it's spawn point should it die before respawning itself again.

Last stage is a race against a slime, if the slime gets to the end first, then the player will die and have to restart.

For the main menu, I added a music library that lets you play any of the 3 songs in the game, if the song is already playing then it won't change, otherwise it will lower the music players volume until it hits 0, then changes the music before bringing it back up so that it doesn't feels so abrupt when the music changes.

Studio Projects

There are 2 studio projects that I feel I have done enough of to mention. 1 is a parody of squid game, the other a rogue like shooter game.


Super Pain

The first project is the game Super Pain, in this project I have made the entire shop system, helped out with the weapon selection (Mainly transition screen to main game as well as a border to highlight the weapons when the player hovers over them), and lastly, I did the map generation so that the rocks and trees are spawned randomly every time to block the players as well as a "Super Pain" power that has a low chance of spawning that will shake the screen and kill every enemy within a certain distance from the player.

To minimize lag and slowdowns I've done distance rendering where if enemies and objects are far enough away from the player, they will not be rendered until they get close enough to the player again.

One extra thing i've added is a way to save the players upgrades they bought from the store, that way they do not have to waste time getting gold again to upgrade from scratch.
